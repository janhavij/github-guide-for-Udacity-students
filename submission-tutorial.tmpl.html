Submitting Your Project On Udacity

An Easy Guide to Getting Your Project on GitHub

This guide will show you step-by-step “How to Submit your Project”. Before you start, Please make sure you have a GitHub account and have uploaded your project on GitHub.
Go to your Udacity portal which looks similar to following.
Click on the plus that is on the left side of the project you want to submit. This will show you the “Submit Project” button along with other information on the project.
Click on the “Submit project” button. This will take you to a screen that looks similar to this:
Click on “Connect with GitHub”. This will take you to the sign-in page for GitHub. Sign in with your GitHub username and password.
Click on “Authorize application” - this means that you are allowing your files on GitHub to be accessed by Udacity.
Next you will be taken to a screen where you pick the project you want to submit.
Choose the one you want to submit and click “Continue”. Next, write a paragraph about your project.
Click “Submit”. This should show you the following.
Congratulations! You have successfully submitted your project.
